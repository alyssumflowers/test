Bee Fun Facts

Did you know that not all bees die after stinging humans once? That only applies to honeybees who have a delicate stinger.
If you allow a honeybee to take their time to dislodge their stinger from your skin, they'll be fine and live a long, happy bee life.
Please be nice to honeybees. We need them as they are our best pollinators.
