Bees

According to all known laws of aviation, a bee should not be able to fly. Bees do not care about the laws of physics and fly anyway.

There are many different kinds of bees. Here are some examples:

  • Honeybees - they make honey
  • Bumblebees - they bumble around
  • Carpenter Bees - they make cool stuff out of wood or something like that

Do not confuse bees for wasps and hornets. Bees are chaotic good to true neutral, wasps and hornets are always chaotic evil. DO NOT MESS WITH WASPS AND HORNETS
